# <cy>Compact Pause Menu</c>

**This mod creates a more organized and compact pause menu interface.**

### <cg>Key Features</c>
* **Integrated Rewards:** Easily access your **Quests** and **Daily Chests** directly from the pause screen.
* **Clutter-Free:** Fully optimized so that all pause menu items and buttons are perfectly placed and easy to navigate.
